In the era of Industry 4.0, CNC machine tool monitoring aims for flexible and adaptive manufacturing operations locally or globally by using integrated information technology (IT) and artificial intelligence (AI). The CNC project aims to develop and demonstrate Industrial 4.0 CNC machining process monitoring and optimization utilizing IIoT and AI capability.
