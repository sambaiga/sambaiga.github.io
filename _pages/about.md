Hello I am **Anthony Faustine** a Senior Industrial Analytics Researcher, at [Irish Manufacturing (IMR), Dublin, Ireland]() where I develop computational models, methods, and tools to help industries and manufacturing  design more effective solutions.  I have experience in applying data science and machine learning techniques to business problems with a track record in the delivery and management of AI projects in academia and industries.

I am also a PhD research at Further Energy and Environment research Laboratory (FEELab), at [ITI/LARSyS](https://iti.larsys.pt/larsys/), [Técnico Lisboa](https://tecnico.ulisboa.pt/en/) in Portugal under [Prof Nuno Jardim Nunes](http://nunonunes.info/) and [Dr. Lucas Pereira](https://www.alspereira.info/) supervision. My Phd research focus on developing robust machine learning algorithms for industrial applications with a specific focus on future energy systems.
