I am **Anthony Faustine**, a Senior Industrial Analytics Researcher at [Irish Manufacturing (IMR), Dublin, Ireland]() where I develop computational models, methods, and tools to help industries and manufacturing design more effective solutions. I have experience applying data science and machine learning techniques to business problems with a track record in the delivery and management of AI projects in academia and industries. Between 2012 and 2017, I worked as an assistant lecturer at the [University of Dodoma](https://www.udom.ac.tz/home), Tanzania, where I was involved in several research projects within ICT for development (ICT4D). In 2017, I joined [IDLab,  imec research group](https://www.ugent.be/ea/idlab/en) of the University of Ghent in Belgium as a Machine learning researcher. From 1st February 2020 to 30th March 2021, I worked as a Data Scientist and Machine learning researcher at [CeADAR (UCD)](https://www.ceadar.ie/), Dublin, Ireland, where I devised and implemented data analytics and AI technical solutions for business problems. 

I am also a PhD research at Further Energy and Environment research Laboratory (FEELab), at [ITI/LARSyS](https://iti.larsys.pt/larsys/), [Técnico Lisboa](https://tecnico.ulisboa.pt/en/) in Portugal under [Prof Nuno Jardim Nunes](http://nunonunes.info/) and [Dr. Lucas Pereira](https://www.alspereira.info/) supervision. My research focuses on developing robust machine learning algorithms for real-world industrial applications, focusing on future energy systems.

My research interest is in time series analysis and computer vision for real-world applications, focusing on Future Energy Systems, Industrial 4.0, and Sustainable Development. 

**Research area**
1. Machine learning and signal processing for time series analysis focusing on energy management, forecasting and predictive maintenance.  
2. Computer vision techniques for machine vision focusing on vision anomaly detection, shape matching and visual inspection. 
4. Artificial Intelligence and Earth Observations(EO)  for sustainability focusing on sustainable energy, agriculture and natural resources management.
5. Data management focuses on specification, strategies, best practices and guidelines for AI dataset development.

I maintain a [repository](https://github.com/sambaiga/AI4DLearning) with resources on learning and applying AI for practical problems.
